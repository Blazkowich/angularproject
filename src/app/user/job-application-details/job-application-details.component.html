Is prior experience in UX design necessary?

Advantage if you have a basic background in design or design software, we are looking for creativity and the ability to learn quickly.

What are the requirements for the role?

Basic understanding of user experience and a desire to learn. Ability to work in a team and communicate well with others. A creative approach and problem-solving mindset.

Will I receive training in the role?

Yes, you will receive training, mentoring, and professional guidance that will help you acquire the knowledge and skills required for the role.

What does a day in this role look like?

Working on screen design for applications or websites, collaborating with developers and product managers, and collecting feedback from users.

What are the challenges expected in the role?

Quick learning of design principles and interfaces, and the ability to balance user requirements with the requirements of the technical team.
